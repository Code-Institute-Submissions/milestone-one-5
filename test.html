Get To Know Us

Jackie | Vocals

Jackie

Jaqui is our amazing vocalist! Being multi-talented she also writes most of the lyrics to our original songs. She loves hard rock and if you close your eyes while listening to our Iron Maiden covers you'd be forgiven for believing Bruce Dickinson was on stage with us!

Patricia | Vocals

Patricia

Patricia is our second amazing vocalist! She handles most of the backups and does an incredible job at that. Also multi-talented she writes some of the lyrics to our original songs. She can also belt it out with the best hard rock vocalists out there and sings lead on some songs.

Fadi 'Freddie' | Drums

Freddie

Fadi 'Freddie' Darwiche is our drummer. It is his relentless pursuit of excellence that is the foundation for our music and upon which the Darwiche show is built. Specializing in aggressive rock beats and complex drumrolls he hits harder than a train!

Gassan 'Gaz' | Lead Guitar

Gassan 'Gaz'

Eddie Van Halen was unavailable, but luckily for us Gassan 'Gaz' is a technical master and can shred with the best of them! He mixes searing shredding passages with haunting riffs, superb phrasing, harmonized melodies and thick, dirty, aggressive rhythms.

Jays | Guitar

Jays

Jays claims he's not a shredder with his style being more melodic and rooted in the blues, but he's got a few tricks to call on when need be. He enjoys using percussive rhythms and playing on dynamics between fretted and open strings all the while mixing solid technique with great phrasing and melodic storytelling.

Rickard 'Bud' | Bass

Bud

Bud is simply a superb bassist. Playing in the pocket and driving the music forward with uncanny precision, rumor has it that he was born with the knowledge and ability to play every single song ever written!
