About

Darwiche is a rock band based in Landskrona Sweden. Originally formed in the 80's the band is made up of four siblings, Fadi 'Freddie' Darwiche, Jaqueline Darwiche, Gassan 'Gaz' Darwiche, Patricia Darwiche and bassist Rickard 'Bud' Linden. The band came back together in 2019!.

After adding guitarist John 'Jays' Traas the band made their second debut at the 2019 Landskrona Festival. Back and writing new original music Darwiche also cover bands such as Iron Maiden, Rainbow, Black Sabbath and many more paying tribute to the guitar bite and the Rard Rock that made us.
