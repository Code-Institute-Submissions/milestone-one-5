DARWICHE

About

Darwiche is a rock band based in Landskrona Sweden. Originally formed in the 80's and made up of members Fadi 'Freddie' Darwiche, Jaqueline Darwiche, Gassan 'Gaz' Darwiche, Patricia Darwiche and Rickard 'Bud' Linden, the band came back together in 2019.

After adding guitarist John 'Jays' Traas the band made their debut at the 2019 Landskrona Festival. Back and writing new music Darwiche also cover bands such as Iron Maiden, Rainbow, Black Sabbath and many more paying tribute to the Rock that made us.
